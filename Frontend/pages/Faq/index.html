Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Listique.

Still need help? Contact us!

How do I create a wishlist?

Sign up and click on the "Create Wishlist" button on your dashboard to get started.

Can I share my wishlist with others?

Yes, share your wishlist via a unique link or directly through social media.

Is Listique free to use?

Yes, Listique offers a free plan with basic features. Premium plans are available for advanced features.

Can I add items from any website?

Yes, add items from any website using our browser extension or manual entry.

How do I manage my wishlist privacy?

Set your wishlist to public, private, or shared with specific people in the settings.

Can I track purchased items?

Yes, mark items as purchased to avoid duplicate gifts.
